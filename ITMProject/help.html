USPC Help Page

The help section of the USPC web portal.

How do I login?

USPC web services use Single Sign On. If you are on your work computer, connected to our LAN or VPN network, then you will be logged in automatically.

How do I find a co-worker?

Visit the "People" page to search or browse a list of USPC employees. Their contact information is found on this page as well.

How do I log research?

Go to the research library and press the plus button to add research.

How do I find my team?

Go to the "Teams" page and look at the assigned team.

What if I have a question?

To ask questions, go to the "discussion" page. You can ask questions regarding research, or the web portal and they can be answered and discussed by all users of the web portal.
